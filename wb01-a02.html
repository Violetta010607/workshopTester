The Ramble Tag

The Ramble Tag is a lightweight harness, designed to be worn on the upper arm of a guide.  It was created to increase pleasure, independence to the blind person, and the guide more confidence while walking together. For more details visit Ramble Tag website.

I have provided you an arm band I have made to give you an idea about how the Ramble Tag looks like.  Take some time to sense it, then wear it on the guide's arm. Have a short walk together.

DIY - Ramble Tag 2.0

Using materials you have received and further materials you may find home, work together to create a personalised low tech. 

What would you add or how would you enhance the Ramble Tag to strengthen your connection?
