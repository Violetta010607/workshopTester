The role AI might play

Activity 3

Silence is also communication

Current voice synthesisers (e.g., Siri) recognise and response to your voice, but they ignore silence. In early research I found silence was used in the sighted guiding practice as a relevant sign to shift the attention towards something more challenging happening around (e.g., while getting through a narrow gap or some passers-by coming up). If a pause in the ongoing conversation while walking together can be detected by an intelligent system an audible feedback could be used to support challenging situations you experience.

Discuss and reflect

What would a sound indicate? How would it support your communication?
