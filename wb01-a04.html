Strengthen the connection

Activity 4

What does a sound tell you about your companion?

In the last activity of this section, I would like to further explore with you how sound can be used to enhance your connection. Move to a comfortable place at home where you usually do not need to use the sighted guiding technique to go around. As in the previous activity, use the running arm band to wear your smartphone on the upper arm of the guide. 

Start the activity close to each other. Then simulate a daily basis task (e.g., open the window, offer a drink) where one of you has to move apart from the other. Do the task and then come back at the starting point. Repeat changing the roles.

Before starting the task, remind to play back a sound of your choice. You can choice one of three below. 

Reflect on your activity

What did the audio feedback tell you about your companion?  Reflect on both cases, when one of you was doing the task and vice-versa. Audio record your answer, upload the audio file, and send it.
