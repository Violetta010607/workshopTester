Activity 1

The sight guiding technique

"Sighted guide technique allows both people on the sighted guide team to feel comfortable, safe, and actively walking together."

I have met several pairs during my study, one of them told me why they enjoy walking together.

Alan (a sighted guide) says: "there is much more going on and travelling with Luke is a great occasion to build up our friendship."

Luke (a person being guided) says: "I enjoy walking with Alan because I can walk at regular pace."
