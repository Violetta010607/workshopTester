The role AI might play

Activity 4

How far are you?

During my studies I have met some pair who told me that while walking together may also happen they physically disengage for some reason (e.g., an unexpected event happening in the surrounding) and both companions may feel disoriented.

Has that ever happened to you? How far do you move? 

A new wearable AI sensor can detect these moments of physical disengagement. Which kind of information would you be interested in receiving?

Try out 2 small applications

Materials you need:

  • Your smartphone.
  • A tangible tag tied to a strap. You can find it in the envelope with a squared marker. A tag is a binary drawing composed of geometric shapes. You can sense it using the large tactile version provided in the materials. 

One person has to hold the smartphone in front of themselves, the other person has to wear the tangible tag on the arm.

The following two pages contain two slightly different applications to detect the tag and when the tag is detected an audible feedback is played back. A brief task description is provided at the top of each page.
