The role AI might play

Activity 4

How far are you?

During my studies I have met some pair who told me that while walking together may also happen they physically disengage for some reason (e.g., an unexpected event happening in the surrounding) and both companions may feel disoriented.

Has that ever happened to you? How far do you move? 

A new wearable AI sensor can detect these moments of physical disengagement. Which kind of information would you be interested in receiving?

Discuss, reflect, and send me a feedback about your experience

What would a sound indicate? How would it support your communication?
