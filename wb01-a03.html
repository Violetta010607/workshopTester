Strengthen the connection

Activity 3

A sound presence

What if the Ramble Tag also produces a sound while walking.

In the envelope marked with a circle there is an armband, which you will be using for the following challenge along with your smartphone.

Probe challenge

Set up the armband and your phone:

  1. Put the hemmed side of the band on the top of guide's upper arm. The arm you usually use to guide your companion. 
  2. Place your phone on the guide's arm and over the armband, so that it's about halfway up the band. The phone speaker should be in the halfway up.
  3. Roll up the bottom of the armband over the phone, so that it holds the phone snuggly.

Get ready for having a short walk outside using the sighted guide configuration. Before taking off play back the sound pressing the button here below. The sound will be played back repeatedly until you stop it. 

Are you back? Stop the audio file.

As in activity 1, audio record your reflection and send the recording using the form here below.
